Borders Logs

The website Borders Logs was a task for a live client. During my studies at university, my given task was to create a website, where I chose to work closely with my father who ran his own log business. As he did not have a way to output all of his offerings for his logs, I decided to assist and create himself a website. The website featured a Shop Page which provided users with a visual of the products available, an About Us Page which delved into behind the scenes of how my father ran his business, and a Testimonials Page which had reviews from a couple of his customers. Lastly, a Contact Us Page which allowed individuals to get in contact easily to arrange a delivery of logs.

Key Features

Mobile Responsive

The website has been constructed through a Bootstrap framework, where a column layout can be utilised to scale the website down when a user wishes to view the website through their mobile device, or other devices.

Contact Form

The Contact Form was included so users had the ability to contact my father to notify him if users required logs. Once the Contact Form was submitted, the response would be delivered directly to my fathers email where he can have direct access to the orders.

JavaScript Integration

JavaScript was included through the logo, which upon arrival appears from the left side of the screen. This gives off the effect that the van and trailer is moving, just like my fathers van and trailer would be when he delivers the logs.
