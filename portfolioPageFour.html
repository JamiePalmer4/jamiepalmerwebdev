Insight

The website Insight was my final year project, which focussed on exploring the effect of accessibility on visually impaired users. The pages provided on the website was the Home Page which gave users a brief understanding of what the website was about, along with links to the different pages on the site. The Vision Check Page allowed end-users to participate in a Vision Test, which gave them a recommendation on whether they should book an appointment with their nearest opticians. The Common Eye Conditions Page gave users an insight into a number of different eye conditions, and their effect. The Visual Challenges Page showcased the daily challenges of individuals with impairment problems, along with assistive technologies that could be used to help manage the problem. Lastly, a Contact Page was made to allow users who wish to get in touch about visual impairments, or if they had any queries about anything related.
