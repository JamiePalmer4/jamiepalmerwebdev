Harry Potter & The Philosophers Stone

The website Harry Potter & The Philosophers Stone was a brief given throughout university, with goals to create a functioning website with Python to display a quiz related to the movie. The pages provided on the website was the Home Page which gave users a brief understanding of what was entailed on the website, along with links to other pages. The About Us Page was provided which described what happened in the movie, such as key events and how the story panned out. The Characters Page displayed the main characters which appeareed in the movie. A Philosophers Quiz Page was implemented to show the quiz and allow users to test their knowledge of the movie. Lastly, a Gallery Page was created to show images from the movie, along with behind-the-scenes shots of how the movie was directed.

Link to Harry Potter website.

Key Features

Python

A Python Quiz related to Harry Potter and The Philosophers Stone was implemented. The user would answer ten questions and the quiz will keep score of the user. The score will be displayed at the end of the quiz, and give users a response depending on the score they achieve.

Terms & Conditions and Privacy Policy

A Terms & Conditions and Privacy Policy was created in order to notify the users of how their data was stored, along with the terms and condition of the website. This gave an outline to users that any data that was collected was stored safe and securely by the website.

Mobile Responsiveness

The website was designed in a column based layout which allowed for the website to be scalable for other devices like mobile phones and tablets, along with desktop and laptops. This means that users will not be greeted with overflowing content upon arrival, making their experience easy and straight forward.
